Catalyst

Meeting fellow makers is an incredible feeling.

Catalyst is a social coding event where teens come together for a weekend to discover the joy of code, build creative projects, and share their projects with the world.

This summer, 175+ hackers will travel around the world to Bangalore, India for an incredible 42-hour hackathon!

There'll be free food, good vibes, and awesome people.

For now, we're working things out, so expect more details.
